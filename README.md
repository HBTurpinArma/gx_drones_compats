# GX - Drones



## Current Features
- Drone inventory framework
  - Adds ability to deploy drones from inside your inventory
  - Keeps track of drone fuel states when picking and deploying them
  - Full compatibility with [ace](https://github.com/acemod/ACE3/blob/master/README.md?plain=1) interaction
  - Ability to refuel drones directly from in your inventory when using ace
  - Additional compatibility for vanilla drones and western sahara drones
- New drones accessible by all sides in editor/zeus and arsenal
  - RQ-11B Raven
  - MQ-8B Fire Scout (Armed, Recon & Seated Variants)
  - Black Hornet 4
  - Drone40 (Recon, HE & Smoke Variants)
  - Honeybadger (AT Mine Variant)
  - ...
- New pylons to be used in the MQ-8B or other assets
  - 2x Missle Rack
  - 12x Rocket Pod
  - Additional compatibility for [ace](https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=463939057) missile/rocket ammo
  - Additional compatibility for [RKSL CVWP](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3612124287) missile/rocket ammo
